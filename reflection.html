ASSIGNMENT 1: POSTER

OVERVIEW OF ICT JOBS IN CLARITY TECHWORKS SDN BHD

The talk by Clarity Techworks Sdn. Bhd. provided an overview of the Information Technology sector. The ICT industry is highly competitive, and professionals must constantly learn and stay updated with the latest advancements to succeed. Careers in ICT offer a wide range of options and are crucial to our daily lives. Nonetheless, the speaker emphasized the necessity of having a foundational understanding for positions like web developer, UI/UX designer, and Android and iOS developer. Gaining greater programming expertise and improving communication skills are essential in order to be in this industry. The talk also emphasized how dynamic ICT occupations are and how important it is to be flexible and agile when navigating a field that necessitates constant learning. IT jobs require a comprehensive strategy that combines academic understanding with real-world application. This talk really motivates me to view problems as opportunities for personal and professional development, understanding that success in the sector necessitates continual learning, practical experience, and effective networking.

ASSIGNMENT 4: NEWSLETTER

TECH-DRIVEN INNOVATION: UNLEASHING THE POWER OF EMERGING TECHNOLOGIES FOR BUSINESS GROWTH IN URBAN FARMING-SOLUTION (I-FARM)

This talk has opened my eyes to how important technology is to humans today.The I-Farm model presented a creative solution for delivering fresh organic produce to communities. I-Farm utilizes IoT into agriculture to supervise and manage farming procedures. IoT also helps in order to supply chain transparency, enabling consumers to trace the origins of their vegetables and farmers to track their shipments. Besides, I-Farm utilizes cloud computing for efficient storage and retrieval of extensive data. Due to COVID-19 Pandemic, I-Farm has shifted to online markets which facilitates user access to a broader range of produce, potentially sourced directly from farms, thereby decreasing environmental impact through optimized resource utilization and reduced waste. To sum up, these technologies should be appreciated as they have facilitated human affairs and simplified our daily tasks.

PC ASSEMBLE

I gained knowledge about the inside components of the PC—which I had never seen before—during the PC assembly session. We were split up into groups of four or five people each to share a single CPU. The CPU was distributed before we reassembled it. The motherboard is then inserted into the CPU frame. We ensure that both components were correctly positioned before locking up. The power supply cable was connected as well to the motherboard. After that, the RAM board was put into the frame. Finally, the VGA card, wire card, and all other cables were connected before the cover case was placed. I also learnt the functions of each components in the CPU and how it works in the system. Thanks to Dr Azurah and all the technicians for giving me this learning experience.
